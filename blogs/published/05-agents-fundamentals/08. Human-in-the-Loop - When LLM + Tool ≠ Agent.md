#### **Introduction to Human-in-the-Loop** 

**HITL** scenarios refer to systems where humans are actively involved in the decision-making process, often working in conjunction with **LLMs** and **Tools**. In these scenarios, the **LLM** and **Tool** are used to support human decision-making, but the human is ultimately responsible for making the decision and taking action.

#### Understanding LLM + Tool ≠ Agent in HITL Scenarios

In HITL scenarios, the LLM and Tool are used to support human decision-making, but the human is ultimately responsible for making the decision and taking action. This means that the LLM and Tool are not autonomous Agents, but rather components of a larger system that requires human intervention to function effectively.
